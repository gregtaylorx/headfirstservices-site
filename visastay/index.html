FAQ

Common questions

How does VisaStay know how many days I can stay?

Each trip is matched against your passport nationality and the destination's visa rules — visa-free duration, visa-on-arrival, e-visa, or visa-required — covering 157 countries and 32+ passports. See full coverage →

Does it handle the Schengen 90/180 rule?

Yes. VisaStay calculates your rolling 90-out-of-180-day Schengen window automatically across every Schengen-area trip, not just a single country at a time. Read the full guide →

Is the visa information legal advice?

No. VisaStay is a planning tool, not a substitute for official guidance. Visa rules change, and you should always confirm current requirements with the relevant embassy or consulate before traveling.
