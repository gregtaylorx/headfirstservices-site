Common questions

How many countries can Norwegian citizens visit visa-free?

Of the 156 destinations tracked in VisaStay, 96 allow Norwegian citizens to enter completely visa-free. Another 48 are accessible via a fast eTA, eVisa, or visa on arrival, and 12 require a visa arranged in advance.

Does the Schengen 90/180-day rule apply to Norwegian citizens?

No. Norwegian citizens have freedom-of-movement rights within the EU/EEA and Switzerland, so the 90/180-day limit that applies to non-EU visitors does not apply here. Read the full Schengen guide →

Is this visa information legal advice?

No. This page is a planning reference, not legal or immigration advice. Visa rules change, and you should always confirm current requirements with the relevant embassy or consulate before you travel.
